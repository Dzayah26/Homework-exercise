Four Large Protests in U.S. History

The tradition of protests that demand progressive change in the United States contiunes.

Here are the country's four of the top protests in terms of attendance:

  1. George Floyd Protest nationwide in 2020 with an estimated 26 million protestors.
  2. Women's March in 2017 drew about 5.6 million people nationwide.
  3. March for Our Lives a student lead protest in 2018 against gun violence, drew about 2 million people.
  4. March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay, and Bi Equal Rights Liberation drew about 1 million people in 1993.
    5. compiled from wikipedia.
